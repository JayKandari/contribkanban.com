Welcome to Contrib Kanban!

This is a huge work in progress created by Matt Glaman

The goal is to provide the Drupal.org community with a simpler way to sprint on projects.

At the moment each project has the same lists, and in the future maintainers will be able to customize these lists by issue state and tags.

Break down of the lists

  • Backlog: Postponed & Needs more info
  • Active
  • Needs Review
  • Needs Work
  • RTBC
  • Fixed: Currently just Fixed, no Closed (fixed) for now.
  • Won't Fix Hidden, due to possible performance/number of calls. Anything closed and not fixed.
